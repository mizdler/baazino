What is Project Euler?
Project Euler is a series of challenging mathematical/computer programming problems that will require more than just mathematical insights to solve. Although mathematics will help you arrive at elegant and efficient methods, the use of a computer and programming skills will be required to solve most problems. For more information on Project Euler, visit http://projecteuler.net/about


Note: If you any problems that you've solved only within the app but not yet on the ProjectEuler.net, they will appear with a yellow tick instead of a green and are also listed under 'Solved Here' list filter. You must manually answer these problems to enter the correct captcha.

Permissions:
This app now requires two permissions:
1. Internet: Your Internet connection will be used to connect to your ProjectEuler.net account, should you decide to use that feature.
2. External Storage: Any new problems that are published after problem number 420 will be downloaded and saved on the external storage.
